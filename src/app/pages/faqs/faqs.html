Basic Questions for Service Providers
1. How do I register as a service provider on SaadiSarthi?

To register, click on the "Become a Provider" button on our homepage and fill out the registration form with your business details, contact information, and service offerings. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 2 business days.

2. What types of services can I offer on SaadiSarthi?

We accept a wide range of service providers including home services (plumbing, electrical, cleaning), tutors, fitness trainers, beauty professionals, event planners, and more. If you're unsure whether your service qualifies, please contact our support team.

3. How does the payment system work?

Payments are processed through our secure platform. Customers pay SaadiSarthi when booking, and we transfer funds to your account (minus our service fee) within 3-5 business days after service completion.

Account & Profile Management

You can update your services and pricing at any time through your provider dashboard. Navigate to "My Services" section where you can add new services, modify existing ones, or adjust your pricing. Changes are reflected immediately on your profile.

Yes, you can specify multiple service areas in your profile settings. You can set different service radii for different locations and even adjust pricing based on travel distance if needed.

The availability calendar in your dashboard allows you to set your working hours, block off unavailable times, and manage appointments. You can sync it with your personal calendar to avoid double bookings.

We understand emergencies happen. You can cancel a booking through your dashboard, but frequent cancellations may affect your provider rating. We recommend canceling at least 24 hours in advance whenever possible.

Complete your profile with detailed descriptions, high-quality photos, and competitive pricing. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews. Maintain high response rates and good ratings to appear higher in search results.
Payments & Commissions

Our standard commission is 15-20% per booking depending on your service category. High-volume providers may qualify for reduced rates. All fees are clearly displayed before you accept each booking.

Payments are processed weekly. After service completion and customer approval, funds (minus our commission) are transferred to your registered bank account every Tuesday. The first payment may take 7-10 days for verification.

There are no hidden fees. Our commission covers platform usage, marketing, and payment processing. Optional premium features like profile boosting may have additional costs, but these are always opt-in.

Customers can tip through the app after service completion. Tips are 100% yours with no commission. They're included in your weekly payout.

We support direct bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS) to Indian bank accounts. International providers can receive payments via PayPal or Wise. All payments are processed in INR.
